Explore The Philippines

The Philippines is an archipelagic country in Southeast Asia. Situated in the western Pacific Ocean, it consists of about 7,641 islands that are broadly categorized under three main geographical divisions from north to south: Luzon, Visayas and Mindanao

One of the Philippines best assets is its 7,000 islands, edged by white sand buffering turquoise waters filled with some of the world's most diverse marine life. And most of them are relatively unexplored.

Philippines' Mountains are underrated.The mountains in the Philippines is varied, and there is definitely a trail for everybody. One of the most definitive experiences provided by mountain hiking and climbing is the views from high above.

Our website's purpose is to promote the beauty of our country, the Philippines. Featuring different beautiful tourist attractions/places that the country can offer.
